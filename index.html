About Me

I am Saksham Raj, a self-made learner and aspiring Chartered Accountant with a strong vision to create impact in finance, education, and entrepreneurship.


Born and raised in a small city where opportunities in commerce were limited, I grew up with very little support, yet developed resilience and self-reliance early in life. At just 15 years old, I began working and walking 8 km daily to support my education and my mother.


My journey has been shaped not by privileges, but by hardship, persistence, and a burning desire to achieve. From teaching students in my locality to pitching startup ideas to investors, I have always believed in creating value through knowledge and innovation.


Today, I am not just a student — I am a future CA, teacher, entrepreneur, and dreamer determined to transform challenges into stepping stones toward success.
